So, what exactly ZingerS is?

Well, we're speaking English, so I think it's no need to explain the meaning of the word zinger as I did it in Russian and Japanese version... But why there's S in the end? Just because Z in the beginning and S in the end look kinda interesting, like you can see on the logo.

And that's how it is. We are a synthpop duet specializing on the light music. The explanation of several wonderful meanings of the word light is no need too, isn't it?

The members of the duet are H1K0 and KicKReprod. H1K0 is in charge of musical staff, KicKReprod is in charge of all the other things. But both are vocalists. ZingerS was founded on January 28, 2019, when KicKReprod sent H1K0 lyrics of the first single «Stay Alive» and suggested creating a duet. There was a time...

Although we have only two completed songs, we continue creating and developing so everything is just beginning!
